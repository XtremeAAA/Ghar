Butter Chicken

Our signature Butter Chicken features tender pieces of chicken cooked in a velvety tomato and butter sauce, enriched with cream and aromatic spices. Marinated overnight in yogurt and spices, the chicken is grilled to perfection before being simmered in the rich sauce. Served with basmati rice or naan bread.

Allergy Information

Contains: Dairy, Tree Nuts (cashew paste)

Paneer Tikka

Cubes of fresh paneer (Indian cottage cheese) marinated in a blend of yogurt, ginger, garlic, and traditional spices. Skewered with bell peppers and onions, then grilled in our tandoor oven until golden brown. Served with mint chutney and fresh lemon wedges. A perfect appetizer or main course for vegetarians.

Allergy Information

Contains: Dairy

Vegetable Biryani

Layered basmati rice cooked with fresh seasonal vegetables, caramelized onions, and a blend of aromatic spices. Prepared using the traditional dum cooking method where the dish is sealed and slow-cooked to perfection. Served with raita (yogurt sauce) and a side of pickle. A fragrant and flavorful rice dish that's a complete meal.

Allergy Information

Contains: Dairy (in raita)
