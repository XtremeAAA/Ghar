Our Story

Ghar, which means "home" in Hindi, is the culinary expression of Depinder Chhibber's passion for authentic Indian home cooking. Drawing inspiration from her grandmother's kitchen in Punjab, Depinder brings generations of family recipes to your table.

After captivating audiences on MasterChef Australia with her heartwarming dishes, Depinder founded Ghar to share the true essence of Indian cuisine - not the restaurant version, but the comforting, nourishing meals made in homes across India.

At Ghar, we believe food is more than sustenance; it's an expression of love, a connection to heritage, and a way to create lasting memories around the table.
